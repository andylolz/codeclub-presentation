One thing you should try to get right:

Make sure you set
final_slide_number = 20
to the final slide number in your presentation.

feeblepoint can’t auto-detect this, because of that file:// thing, and it’s dangerous to have a presentation that behaves differently off your USB than it does off the webserver. It’s a feature.

But if you get this wrong, you can still punch through to the next slide with the enter key anyway.
